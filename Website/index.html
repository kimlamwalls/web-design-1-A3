About Ivy Mae

Ivy Mae is an emerging professional photographer, deeply committed to expanding her already impressive portfolio. With a keen eye for the aesthetic and a vibrant creative spirit, Ivy is dedicated to capturing the essence of her subjects. Her work ranges across a broad range of disciplines, but she holds a special passion for fashion photography, studio still life, and expressive portraiture. With a background in fine arts and a passion for exploring photographic imagery, Ivy brings an enthusiastic perspective to each project, and is able to provide her clients with memorable and meaningful photographs.
